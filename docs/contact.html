By Bus

From Chengannur, board a bus to Kota, Pathanamthitta. Alight at SN College, Mezhuveli bus stop.

By Road

Follow Minnesota Highway 23 into St. Cloud. Cross the Mississippi River on the Granite City Crossing Bridge. Turn south on Fifth Avenue South. Turn east on Sixth Street South. Public parking is available in the 4th Avenue Parking Ramp, N lot (pay area) and the Miller Center. Please follow the signage posted in each lot.
